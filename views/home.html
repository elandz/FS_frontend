About Us

FriendSend is a door-to-door shipping service available 24/7 every day of the year.
You can send absolutely anything, anywhere - within your city, from one city to another or to the other side of the world.
It's cheap, convenient, and a lot of fun.

How it Works



1. Create A Shipment Request

Let us know what, when, and where it needs to go and then... wait for it. It's that simple!



2. Select A Courier

We will find people within the FriendSend community whom are travelling in the same direction as your stuff.



3. Have It Delivered To Your Door

Meet the courier at a gas station, parking lot or have your stuff delivered to the front door.
It's that convenient!
